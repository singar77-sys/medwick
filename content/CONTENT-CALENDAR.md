# Medwick Construction: Content Calendar

Publishing plan for the rebuild. Sequenced to the audit's storm calendar: the build window (mid-July → mid-October 2026) sits inside peak wind season (August is busiest) and rides the September "roof repair near me" national peak. Winter leak/pipe content must index **before** the January leak-repair spike, so it ships by late October.

## Priority order (audit §4.7)

1. **Insurance-claim guide hub:** the verified-empty niche. Highest strategic value.
2. **Localized cost guides:** the format 4K proved ranks.
3. **Emergency answer content:** "flooded basement: who to call," burst-pipe first hour, emergency tarping.
4. **Storm-response posts:** within 48h of NWS-warned events (4K's proven accelerator).
5. **Project case studies:** every job → site + GBP + social.
6. **Winter content:** roof leaks, ice dams, frozen pipes. Live by late October.

## Cadence

- **Guides/cornerstone:** ~1 substantive guide per week through the build, then ~2-3/month sustained.
- **Case studies:** every completed job (target ≥1/week once crews are producing photo sets).
- **Storm-response:** event-triggered, not scheduled: playbook pre-drafted and ready to publish in 24-48h.
- **Social:** 3 posts/week (1 completed project, 1 in-progress/crew, 1 educational), every post geo-tagged. Same assets feed GBP photos + posts.

## Storm-response playbook (pre-built, event-triggered)

Ready-to-fire template so response is measured in hours, not days:
- Blog post: "Hit by the {date} {hail/wind} storm in {towns}? Here's what to do." (4K's exact winning play.)
- GBP post mirroring it + updated photos.
- Ad copy variant (only if the ads test is live).
- Trigger: any NWS-warned severe event in the county. Peak months March-November; August busiest. Lead with **wind + aging-roof** messaging: the last widespread large hail was March 2020.

## Seasonality anchors (published on-page)

"roof repair near me" peaks September · "roof leak repair" peaks January · "emergency roof repair" peaks December · broad roofing demand surges March-October. Time cost/repair content to September; winter content live by late October to index ahead of January.

## The one-asset-three-channels rule

Every job produces: a photo set + short video + one homeowner takeaway → website case study + GBP post + social posts. This is the only content engine a two-crew company can actually sustain: it is the plan, not a nice-to-have.
