# Medwick Construction: 2026 Strategic Positioning

**Purpose:** The single business strategy behind everything else, and the north star for the website, content, SEO, and marketing. Where a tactical document and this positioning disagree, this wins.

> **Medwick is not competing to become another roofing company.** The opportunity is larger. The company should position itself as **Medina County's Insurance Restoration Advocate.** Roofing, water mitigation, and reconstruction are services. Insurance advocacy is the brand.

## The market reality

Three distinct competitive landscapes:

- **Roofing** is mature and fragmented. Several companies own individual pieces of authority (local trust, certifications, reviews, longevity, digital marketing), but no company owns all of them.
- **Water mitigation** is the weakest market: dominated by national franchises, very little local authority, almost no educational content, little AI visibility. No company owns the "trusted local advocate" position.
- **Remodeling** is highly competitive, well-established, and credential-driven. A long-term opportunity, not an immediate focus.

## The discovery

The owner interview changed the strategy. Asked what Medwick actually does during an insurance claim, the answer was not about construction. It was:

- walk the homeowner through the claim
- help make the insurance calls
- meet the adjuster
- prepare a complete, documented repair estimate and meet the adjuster to walk through the proposed scope of work
- if the adjuster's scope misses damage we've documented, show our photos and measurements and provide a detailed repair estimate
- coordinate the repairs
- complete the roof, perform mitigation, and rebuild if necessary

This is not three businesses. It is one continuous process.

## The new position

| Existing position | Recommended position |
|---|---|
| Roofing + Water Mitigation + Reconstruction | **Insurance Restoration Advocacy** |

Everything else supports this.

**Brand promise:** We don't just repair your property. We guide you through one of the most stressful experiences you will ever face, from the first insurance phone call to the final repair.

## Core brand pillars

1. **Insurance advocacy (the flagship).** We answer to you, the homeowner. We prepare a complete, documented repair estimate and meet the adjuster to walk through the proposed scope of work. We explain every step. We stay until the project is finished. No competitor owns this position.
2. **Complete storm recovery.** One company, one relationship, the entire project: roof, then water mitigation, then reconstruction. No local competitor provides this complete path while remaining locally owned.
3. **Local identity.** Medina born, Brunswick raised. The Medwick name comes from MEDina + BrunsWICK. Authentic community roots are hard to copy.
4. **Communication.** Customer feedback repeatedly highlights responsiveness, clear explanations, accessibility, and a personable experience. This is culture, not marketing. Lean into it.
5. **Documentation.** Every project becomes an asset. Every completed job generates before and after photos, drone photos, a homeowner testimonial, a Google review, a case study, a GBP post, social content, and FAQ material. Nothing leaves without documentation.

## Primary audience

Not people looking for roofing. People experiencing property damage. Typical searches: roof leaking, tree hit roof, basement flooded, insurance denied claim, burst pipe, hail damage, emergency tarp, ceiling water stain, mold concerns. **Own the problem, not merely the solution.**

## Website philosophy

The website is not a brochure. It is an authority engine. Every page answers exactly one homeowner question, with a direct answer, an educational explanation, local relevance, trust signals, and a call to action. Repeat hundreds of times.

## Navigation

Organize by the homeowner's problem, not by trade, and move insurance high:

`Storm Damage · Insurance Claims · Roofing · Water Mitigation · Reconstruction · Service Areas · Projects · Learning Center · About · Contact`

## SEO philosophy

Do not try to immediately outrank legacy competitors for "roofing Medina" or "roof replacement Medina." Dominate hundreds of smaller decision-stage searches instead: does insurance cover hail damage, how long before mold grows, what should I do after a roof leak, will insurance pay for water mitigation, should I tarp my roof, can I choose my own contractor, tree fell on house what now, wet drywall replacement, emergency water removal. This builds authority rather than chasing keywords.

## AI search strategy

Become the company AI systems naturally cite. Every answer should solve one homeowner problem, contain first-hand expertise, include local context, explain the insurance process, and demonstrate authority. The long-term goal is citation dominance, not traditional ranking alone.

## Competitive advantage

Competitors sell services. Medwick sells confidence. The customer should think: "I don't have to figure this out anymore." That emotional shift is the moat, because a competitor cannot copy it by redesigning a homepage. It requires changing what the company is, not just how it markets itself.

## Long-term vision (five years)

Recognized throughout Medina County, with a physical office, expanded staff, and community involvement. Known for insurance advocacy, trusted as storm-restoration experts, and the strongest local educational resource.

## Immediate priorities

- **Build:** authority website, insurance-claim hub, storm-damage hub, water-mitigation hub, project portfolio, resource center.
- **Collect:** reviews, photography, videos, testimonials, case studies.
- **Publish:** educational guides, insurance content, emergency content, storm updates, local project stories.
- **Earn:** IICRC certifications, additional manufacturer relationships, local awards, community recognition.

## Strategic warning

Do not become another contractor website. There are already dozens. Become the trusted guide homeowners seek when disaster strikes. That position is far more defensible and aligns directly with the owner's actual strengths.

## Positioning statement

> **Medwick Construction is Medina County's Insurance Restoration Advocate.** We guide homeowners from the first insurance phone call through the final repair, providing roofing, water mitigation, and reconstruction under one trusted local company. We don't simply repair homes. We help people recover.

> Medwick Construction is a licensed general contractor, not a licensed public insurance adjuster. We document damage, prepare repair estimates, and coordinate and perform repairs. We do not negotiate, adjust, or interpret your insurance policy or claim settlement. For claim negotiation or coverage questions, consult your insurer, a licensed Ohio public adjuster, or an attorney. Our compensation is for the construction work performed, not a percentage of your insurance claim.
